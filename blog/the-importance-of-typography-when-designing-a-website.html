The importance of typography

Joshua Matambo
|
October 15, 2020

Typography is something no one, probably never think of when browsing the internet unless the website happens to be messy and unreadable but typography is a very important tool used in web design. Good typography can make text pleasant to read and easier to do a quick browse through since we live in a time when people don’t have time nor patience to struggle through your website to find information that they can obtain elsewhere easier.

Typography is an artform which uses font, font size, layout, and other techniques to make text more readable and appealing when displayed. When using typography, they are a few things you can consider:

The first step is font selection, the correct font should be able to become part of the atmosphere that is created when viewing the website. Some fonts carry a more formal weight when used and some a more exciting weight, this should all be considered when choosing a font. Your project should on average have no more than three different fonts or it starts to look untidy and uniform thus giving your website the feeling that it was made in a rush by an amateur.

The size of your characters is also important, a general rule is that the larger you make your text the more attention you are calling to that piece of text. You should also practise restraint because making the text to large will overshadow everything else and become more difficult to read. I recommend you only use a larger font size to call attention to heads and other key pieces of information like your business motto.

White space, this is the space between your characters whether it is the space between rows of text or the space between individual letters. Sometimes when writing long paragraphs, it is good practise to add a little white space to make it easier to look at and read. For heading and titles where you may use a bold font reducing the whitespace adds a gravity like effect that make it seem as if the letters are pulling towards each other.
