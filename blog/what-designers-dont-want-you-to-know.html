What Designers Don't want you to know

Michael Matambo
|
September 9, 2020

In this article, I'm going to talk about finding a web designer that will help grow your business. Remember that a website is never done, and finding the right web designer is key to growing your business.

‍

Tip #1: You don't always have to redesign your website

Depending on the technology used to develop your website, a redesign might not be necessary. 

If the website was developed using WordPress, in most cases, that website would need to be redesigned as WordPress websites are known for always breaking and don't have the cleanest code. 

If the website's code is good, then a refresh with new images, content structure would be needed—no need for a redesign.

‍

Tip #2: A Website is never a one-off project

Most web designer likes to charge a one-time fee for a website redesign and hand over the project to the client. Changes need to be done constantly on a website. It's good to have a Web Designer that will not only redesign your website but will manage your website for a monthly fee. You won't have to worry if the website is up to date. That's the job of your Web Designer. A ''good'' website is a website that helps your business to grow by generating new leads and sales every month. 

‍

Tip #3: Making small updates doesn't take time

Making small changes to a website should not take time. Many Web Designers are known for making you wait for ages. 

Web Designers that charge a monthly fee for maintaining and updating a website are the best as they are quick to make changes as they understand how small changes can make a big impact. 

Making small updates to a website should not take more than 8 hours.

‍

Tip #4: You might not need a website but a Ecosystem

If your Web Designer stops the work when the website is designed, they haven't finished the job yet.

Your website should have tools to automate emails, marketing tools that all are in sync and work together. Your Web Designer should integrate the following to your website:

  1. Social Media Accounts
  2. Email Automation
  3. Conversion Tracking tools
  4. Lead generation tools

All the above integration will tremendously grow your business as you can track where your leads are coming from and create a re-targeting strategy.

‍

Conclusion

Most web design companies do good work but find a web design company that does an exceptional job. 

Web design companies that not only redesign your website but also manage and integrate it with other tools.
