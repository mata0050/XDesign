Why you are leaving money on the table?

Joshua Matambo
|
September 22, 2020

IF you do not have a website or have a poor looking and poor performing website, you could be leaving thousands of dollars on the table. A good website is an insight into your business. It is where customers can easily find information about you, gain access to you, and experience your brand.

‍
Websites can also create new opportunities to engage with your customers and provide them with better service. Websites can be used to advertise new products, show what promotions you are having and give you access to more customer data such as email addresses so as to create a more personal connection with each customer. Other benefits that could more directly impact your bottom line include online retailing so that you do not have to rely solely on third-party applications to sell your products or services.

‍

Having a website makes it easier to have a place to send customers after they engage with your online adverts and can fully experience your brand. A website allows you access to more than just the local market; it provides you access to a broader market, whether it be the global market or only your national market. Websites allow you to scale your business to almost 100% capacity utilization before investing more capital to expand the physical infrastructure. A website makes you look professional, thus making it more attractive to prospective employees allowing you to increase your chances of getting the best employees in the job markets.

‍
Website make it easier for prospective new clients to find you with a simple google search, whether it is your business they are directly searching for or the products you sell that they are searching for.
Having your website allows you to do things like this, writing a blog to give your stakeholders information about your industry and provide information to those curious about what you do. It is a great way to create interest in your industry and also strengthen your brand.

‍

All of the things I have talked about help increase the customer experience. Making your website simple and easy to navigate also improves customer service due to the ease at which the customer can extract information from the website.
