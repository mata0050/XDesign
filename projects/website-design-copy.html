Strategy to our Website Design Process

Discovery

Stage 1

We learn more about your company's goals, objectives, ideal outcome and site content.

Wireframe

Stage 2

Using the ideas discovered in stage 1, Our team build the general flow of your site by visualizing the structure clearly.

Design

Stage 3

Our team use artistic expression and imagination to bring the wireframe to life with a professional design.

Development

Stage 4

Development of designed pages in Webflow; CMS installation; testing and sign-off.

Launch

Stage 5

Development of responsive versions (mobile and tablet); website launch; training session.

Maintenance

Stage 6

Our team asses your needs for on-going support and maintenance.
